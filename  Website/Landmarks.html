Iconic and historic landmarks

Princess of hope

A natural rock formation which resembles a women gazing in the sky. This was named by Angelina Jolie

Balochistan Sphinx

This natural formation resembles Egyptian sphinx. This structure was naturally built by erosion which makes it truly a wonder. This is a great and memorable picture spot for history lovers

Tomb of Hani

This tomb is associated with a amazing love story in balochi folkore. This tomb has a great cultural significance and is also a site of pilgrimage for some locals.

Omani Fort (Old Portuguese Fort)

This fort is a historic military structure built when it was under the control of Oman.This fort was used to defend the port with its thick solid walls and great views of the sea which shows the intelligent architecture

Mud Volcanoes (Chandrakup)

Gwadar has one of the few active cold mud volcanoes of the world. For some pilgrims it is also considered sacred. This place is very photogenic at the same time

Jiwani Wetlands

Located in the west of Gwadar, this place is home to endangered species. It contains mangroves for nature lovers and migratory birds can also be observed over here for tourists which are bird watchers

Activities to Do in Gwadar

Gwadar is the perfect place for fun activities whether you are a nature enthusiast or someone who wants adventure you. Here are some of the activities you can do:

  • Beach Bonfires & Camping: The great windy weather of Gwadar is perfect for camping at night and stargazing
  • Snorkelling & Scuba Diving: You can explore the coral reefs and the astonishing world under the water
  • Boating & Jet Skiing: The calm waves at Sur Bandar can be enjoyed with boating and jet skiing perfectly
  • Hiking & Rock Climbing: You can try one of the most challenging treks and hiking in Hingol region of Koh-e-Batil.
  • Birdwatching: You can witness one of the most exotic and endangered species of birds in the region of Jiwani
  • Cultural Tours: Get to know about Balochi culture, food, and traditional music by doing cultural tours
