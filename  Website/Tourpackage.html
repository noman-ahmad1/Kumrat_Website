‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  1. Can I use a drone for photography or videography in Gwadar?
    Usually, drone usage is not allowed in Gwadar due to presence of military zones and other sensitive areas. Although you can be granted permission form local or civil authorities. Flying drones without permission can result in heavy fines of confiscation in some cases.
  2. Can I swim in the sea at Gwadar?
    Swimming is allowed in designated beaches depending on the condition of water. As lifeguards are not present most of the time, dangerous currents can cause fatalities. Always:
    1. Swim in shallow water
    2. Go with groups
    3. Avoid swimming after dark(after 5 pm)
    4. Follow your local guides' advice
  3. Is Gwadar suitable for family trips or solo travellers?
    Absolutely! Gwadar is very safe for both families and solo travellers. However, however, you should still choose a experienced local guide or tour group for the best experience
  4. What is the local currency and are card payments accepted?
    The local currency is the Pakistani Rupee (PKR). Mostly, hotels and restaurants don't accept card payments so keeping cash is the best option. Tourists should exchange their money from money exchanges available on airports or their bus stops.
  5. Is English widely spoken in Gwadar?
    The basic communication in Gwadar is done in Urdu and Balochi. Although some locals can understand and speak English, but you should use translation apps and learn some common Urdu phrases.
